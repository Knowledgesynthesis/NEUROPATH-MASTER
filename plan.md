# **7. NEUROPATH REASONING FRAMEWORK**

1. Determine anatomic region (cortex, cerebellum, brainstem, meninges, nerve)  
2. Assess cell types (glial, neuronal, mixed, lymphoma-like, metastatic)  
3. Evaluate architecture & cytology  
4. Identify signature features (rosettes, whorls, necrosis, inflammation)  
5. Apply IHC conceptually  
6. Apply molecular concept (IDH, 1p/19q, BRAF, INI1, H3)  
7. Integrate into a broad tumor/disease category  
8. Re-check for pitfalls  

Pitfalls:  
- Gliosis vs glioma  
- Reactive meningothelial hyperplasia vs meningioma  
- Lymphoma vs GBM  
- Demyelination mimicking tumor  
- Pediatric embryonal tumors overlap
